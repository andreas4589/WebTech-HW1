Welcome to web for cooking — a journey through flavors and history.

Cooking is more than just making meals — it's a story told through ingredients, flavors and traditions. At web for cooking, we explore the rich history behind the ingredients you use every day while sharing delicious recipes that bring them to life.
Discover the origins of your favorite spices, the cultural significance of staple foods, and how different cuisines have evolved over time. Whether you're here to try a new dish or learn about the history of an ingredient, we invite you to savor the stories behind every bite.

Let's cook, learn and celebrate food together!

Culinary news of the month

What to cook in February

February may be the heart of winter, but it's also a great time to cook with fresh, seasonal ingredients that bring warmth and comfort to your table. Root vegetable like carrots, parsnips, and sweet potatoes are at their peak, perfect for roasting or adding to hearty stews. Leafy greens such as kale and Swiss chard provide a boost of nutrition, while citrus fruits like oranges and grapefruits add a refreshing brightness to both sweet and savory dishes.

If you still don't know wich dish is right for you, DELICIOUS is here to help! On their website, you'll find a variety of meals perfect for the month of February, featuring seasonal ingredients and comforting flavors.

If you're still hesitant, don't worry—we've picked three delicious recipies from their collection that we absolutely approve of. Give them a try and enjoy the best flavors this season has to offer!

Rhubarb, rose and pistachio crumble
This easy to make yet sophisticated dish offers a delightful balance of tart rhubarb and fragrant flavors, perfect for a comforting treat
Lime marmalade
This easy-to-follow recipe captures the essence of fresh limes, resulting in a golden-hued marmalade that's both sweet and tart, making it a delightful addition to your pantry
Speedy anchovy and broccoli pasta
Packed with the savory taste of anchovies and the freshness of broccoli, this budget-friendly recipe come together quickly, making it a go-to meal when you want something tasty without breaking the bank. A perfect balance of salty, tangy, and wholesome!

Street food festival the 21/02 in Amsterdam

If there is one date in february that a foodie should not forget, it's of course the deck Streetfood festival. It's a two hour experience where you will discover street food inspired dishes from all over the world. Don't wait and book a table!
