Web For Cooking

Welcome to web for cooking — a journey through flavors and history.

Cooking is more than just making meals — it's a story told through ingredients, flavors and traditions. At web for cooking, we explore the rich history behind the ingredients you use every day while sharing delicious recipes that bring them to life.
Discover the origins of your favorite spices, the cultural significance of staple foods, and how different cuisines have evolved over time. Whether you're here to try a new dish or learn about the history of an ingredient, we invite you to savor the stories behind every bite.

Let's cook, learn and celebrate food together!

Culinary news of the month

The perfect dish for Valentine's day

Valentine's day is comming and you still have no idea for your diner? Don't worry, delish is here to save your romantic evening.
In their website you can find a diverse array array of recipies, from classic dishes like Chiken Alfredo pasta to more adventurous options such as Mediterranean Pizza and Lobster Risotto. Each recipe is crafted to be accessible, ensuring that even novice cooks can impress their loved ones without stress. The guide also offers suggestions for vegetarian or vegan options, catering to a wide range of tastes and dietary preferences. With this collection, Delish aims to make Valentine's day special by bringing restaurant-quality dining experiences into the comfort of your home.

You're still hesitating? Here some of the recipies that we have tried and recommend without a doubt:

Lobster Ravioli
Tender pasta stuffed with sweet, juicy lobster, all smothered in a creamy, buttery sauce.
—Pure comfort and luxury in every bite.
Italian MeatLoaf
Juicy, flavorful beef mixed with herbs, garlic, and cheese, baked to perfection, and topped with a rich tomato sauce for a cozy, homemade classic.
Indian Butter Chikpeas
Who say that a meal has to have meat to enjoy it? This creamy and flavorfull dish will certainly change your mind about vegetables

Street food festival the 21/02 in Amsterdam

If there is one date in february that a foodie should not forget, it's of course the deck Streetfood festival. It's a two hour experience where you will discover street food inspired dishes from all over the world. Don't wait and book a table!
